About Me

To properly introduce myself, my name is Bob Smith, I am 22 years of age with a degree in Graphic Design.
I come from Glasgow, Scotland and I graduated from Glasgow Caledonian University after 4 years.
Throughout school, I was always very artistic and creative, and as my final school year ended I knew it's what I wanted a career in.
I am very passionate about my work and profession, there is never enough to learn, there's always new challenges and new ways of doing things.


My goals for the future are to eventually have my own self sustaining business where I provide graphic design soloutions to those in need of a design
I also aim to one day be apart of a conglomerate company creating and designing logos and images for the business and be a part of an internal audit team.
In the world of graphic design, pushing yourself is how you become a better designer; doing the things you aren't fully confident about, and nudging out of your comfort zone.
I pride myself in being able to go past that subconscious limit and furthering myself as a designer and at the same time make my designs what they are.
