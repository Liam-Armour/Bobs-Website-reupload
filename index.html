Welcome

The expression through this website aims to give an insight as to what I can do and what I am capable of as a graphic designer.
This is in the hope of gaining a wider spectrum of clientele or furthering my chances of being employed at a position of interest.



Graphics design has been something I have loved doing since I left high school. I knew that I wanted a career in the subject.
Every day for me is still a day of learning something new and working on how I could improve my designs, I am a firm believer that there's always more to learn.
I hope that you utilise this website to really find out more about me as a person and the designs I enjoy doing, and hope that this provides a high standard showcase of what I am capable of.
